JS Motion Tracking Experiment

A hack-per-day by Anthony Bau
Approve use of your webcam, and swipe left/right/up/down in midair to navigate.

Usage Tips

  • Things work best with your hand one keyboard-length away from your webcam
  • Make your palm face the direction of motion (hand perpendicular to screen)
  • One motion should take about the time it takes to exhale (3/4 of a second)
  • Remember when you are moving that your arm and shoulders are in frame, and might be registered as gestures.

About

  • The motion tracker takes the absolute difference between consecutive frames
  • Computes the tracked object as the centroid of pixels that changed
  • Gestures are contiguous blocks of movement that are not interrupted by a 200ms wait
